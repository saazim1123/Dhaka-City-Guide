Hotels In Dhaka

Here is a list of some of the recommended Hotels in Dhaka

Amari Hotel

The Amari Dhaka has a great ambience and highly trained staff. Taj the night manager, resolved an issue very promptly, and efficiently, and is a credit to the Hotel.

Radisson Blu

Radisson Dhaka is located on the main road and is near to Airport. The hotel is property of Dhaka Army and therefore it is a very safe place to stay. The property is spread in more than 18 acres and is having beautiful swimming pool. The rooms are comfortable but the tariff is very high and not recommended for leisure travelers. Services provided are good if not excellent

The Westin

I stayed in Westin Hotel on my visit to Bangladesh for the 16th Asian Art Biennale. This luxury hotel is the perfect place to stay in Dhaka according to my experience. The staff is friendly and the services are wonderful. They have all the facilities hence they are a 5 star rated hotel. The food is excellent and the spread of cuisines are marvellous. I highly recommend this hotel to anyone who visits Dhaka as a must try.

Hotel Sarina

Recently stayed at Hotel Sarina and was pleasantly surprised at the level of service, the comfortable rooms, and the great food. As a business traveler, it's not often that I see a dedicated and proactive manager, like Mr. Paul. He was very welcoming and attentive, and ever present to ensure quality of service and that staff were very responsive to guests' needs.
